Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cornerstone Beauty Group LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit cornerstonebeautygroup.com, contact us, request support, purchase products or services, enroll in educational programs, inquire about professional equipment, or work with us for consulting services.

Purchases and services are provided by Cornerstone Beauty Group LLC.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing or billing address, company name, professional role, license or business information when relevant to professional-use products, inquiry details, order information, support requests, and communications with us.

If you purchase from us, payment details may be collected and processed by our payment processor. We do not intentionally store complete credit card numbers on this website.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide customer support, process orders, deliver products or services, manage educational program enrollment, evaluate professional equipment inquiries, communicate policy updates, prevent fraud, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, and improve our website and services.

Payment Processing

When payments are offered, payment transactions may be processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Those providers process payment information according to their own privacy and security practices. We use payment-related information to confirm orders, provide receipts, support customers, manage refunds, and address payment disputes.

Third-Party Services

We may use service providers for website hosting, payment processing, email, customer support, analytics, order fulfillment, shipping, professional services, and business operations. This website also loads Google Fonts from Google-hosted domains. These providers may process limited information as needed to provide their services.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, payment processors, shipping carriers, professional advisors, regulatory or legal authorities when required, or as needed to protect our rights, customers, business, and services.

Wellness and Professional Information

Please do not submit sensitive medical information through our contact form or email. Our website, products, programs, and services are not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prescription products, or guaranteed results.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, support customers, maintain business records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our policies. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and applicable requirements.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements. You may also opt out of non-transactional marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages or contacting us directly.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No website, email system, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website, products, educational programs, professional equipment, and consulting services are intended for adults and business users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
